Project Title

Welcome to my VFX Showreel!

This reel is a collection of the projects I developed throughout my studies at IAC, showcasing a hybrid workflow of 3D simulation, visual effects, compositing, and generative AI integration.

Core Focus & Skills:

  • Pyro, Vellum, Particles and Fluid Simulations
  • Technical Compositing & Elements Integration
  • Generative AI Workflows & Detail Enhancement

Software & AI Tools Used: SideFX Houdini, Adobe After Effects, Photoshop, and Magnific AI.

I am a VFX artist, a compositor, and a cinematic AI creator for both videos and images. I studied at IAC (Israeli Animation College).

I work in SideFX Houdini, After Effects, Magnific Ai and Photoshop, with a focus on pyro, particles, vellum and fluid simulations.

My inspiration comes from movies, TV shows, and video game cinematics. I enjoy “invisible VFX”—blending digital elements into real footage so perfectly that they look like a natural part of the world, without anyone noticing.

Outside of work, I love watching movies and tv-show’s, playing video games, and going out with friends.
